ISSUE #3

March 26, 2025. 15:01

"EDCDYSIS"

Started this issue earlier because this one is going to cover a lot more. As time passes by, I get to realise that time is the only essential element to express your ideas as clearly as possible.

The language we speak today limits the expression of my thoughts. Hence, I feel grateful to utilize instruments I possess dearly such as my art skills through photographic, musical, audiovisual and painting formats. I have this feeling there is a barrier in front of me to break through. And to breach that wall, I have to experiment, fail and experiment. And then fail again. That is how we evolve, we are on this constant experimentation. Innovation is failure. This is the third issue and probably not the last. Even though I want to get recognition through my art, that is not my first purpose. The purpose is to connect with those who share the same thoughts and feelings as me, and to invite those who never had a chance to pause and take a deep breath for themselves.
