"We don't create by being alive, but we do it by being conscious."
WHILE I'M CONSCIOUS (WIC) is a media atelier founded by Seungyu Jung Jeon (aka The Uncertified Publisher)
What started as the title of my personal journal that I keep on my notes app, it started to branch out and touch upon different art formats. WIC is aiming to highlight artists that bring to the table unspoken narratives through their creative mediums.
